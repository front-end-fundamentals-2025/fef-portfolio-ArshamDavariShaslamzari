about me

My journey in technology began with a solid academic foundation in Electronic Engineering, where I spent two years exploring the world of electronics. Later, I transitioned into Industrial Electric systems, specifically working with machinery and Building Management Systems (BMS), gaining over a year of hands-on experience.

These experiences, combined with my passion for technology, led me to pursue an academic career in Informatics. My growing interest in Web Development expanded to exploring the exciting realms of blockchain programming and the vast opportunities of Web 3.0. I believe that blockchain technology is shaping the future of the internet, and I am eager to dive deeper into this space.
